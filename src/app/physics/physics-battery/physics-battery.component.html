Battery, don't hurt me
Dionne stares at the charging brick rated 25W, then at the phone battery rated 5000mAh, then back at the charging brick again.

It would make much more sense if the battery capacity was measured in joules instead, she thought.

If so, the capacity could easily be calculated by multiplying wattage with charging time.

As we will uncover later, there turns out to be a rather convoluted way to indirectly convert mAh to J.
Before attempting to convert mAh to J, Dionne wanted to first estimate the battery capacity in joules.

Starting with a battery percentage of 12% and using the 25W charger, Dionne records that it takes approximately 40 minutes to fully charge.

Can you estimate the battery capacity in joules? (Rounded to nearest thousand)
68,182J => approx 68,000J
