A Primer in Kinematics
Kinematics is a branch of physics that studies the motion of bodies.

An apple falling from a tree, a satellite orbiting the Earth, an Olympic sprinter running the 100m dash - these are all bodies in motion whose geometries can be described and predicted with kinematics. Thus, kinematics is often referred to as the "geometry of motion".

In this lesson, selected fundamental topics in kinematics will be taught.
